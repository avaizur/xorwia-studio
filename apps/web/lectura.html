Why Students & Professionals Choose Lectura?

Lectura is more than just a recorder. It is an agentic note-taking assistant that bridges the gap between spoken word and professional documentation. Whether you are in a lecture hall, a boardroom, or a legal briefing, Lectura ensures you never miss a beat.

Live Speech-to-Text Accuracy

Using the latest Web Speech APIs, Lectura provides instantaneous feedback. You can watch your words appear on screen with minimal latency, allowing you to focus on the conversation rather than your keyboard.

AI-Powered Notes Enhancement

The true power of Lectura lies in its integration with Amazon Bedrock. Once your transcription is complete, our Claude 3 Haiku model analyzes the raw text to generate structured, professional notes complete with headings, bullet points, and action items.

Multi-Language Mastery

Lectura supports over 10 global languages including English, Spanish, French, German, and Arabic. Transcribe and enhance notes in your native tongue or use it to assist in language learning environments.
